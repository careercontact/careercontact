F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why choose CareerContact?

    For students and schools - authentic, problem-based learning

    on top of basic exposure to digital skills and entrepreneurship.

    For SMEs - fair pricing, no inflated costs*

    (*In fact, it is free at the moment if your request is handled in June or Nov - Dec)
    We are invested in helping small enterprises grow (both in Singapore and beyond)


  • How did CareerContact come about?

    We started with just webinars during the lockdown in Singapore in May 2020

    in a bid to provide career exposure to pre-university students. We have pivoted from just exposure to a more concrete learning outcomes - while we are fully aware that (1) SMEs might not be inclined to take pre-university students due to lack of skills; (2) students may prefer bigger brand names instead of smaller enterprises, we firmly believe that students can meaningfully contribute to digital transformation of enterprises

    Read more

  • How do I sign up?

    We are currently handling our pilot batch (13 Nov - 23 Dec 2020). We are exploring a batch in January and will open sign ups soon - follow our social media for more updates!
